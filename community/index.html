SECTOR'S SATURDAY CHAMPION DISCORD ROLE

Occasionally during Sector's Saturdays, when R2D2Vader is available, we crown one or multiple champion(s) based on performance over one match. The role is purely cosmetic, but helps to keep things interesting.

COMPETITIVE SCENE

The community hosts two seasonal tournaments every year. A summer tournament, usually taking place in late July, and a winter tournament, in the middle of December. You can find more information in the Competitive page.
